My CNC background changed how I build interfaces.

## Shared Principles

- Tolerances matter: in frontend this means spacing systems and predictable states.
- Toolpaths matter: in UI this becomes user flow and interaction sequences.
- Material constraints matter: on the web this means performance budgets and device capability.

## Why It Helps

When you treat interface construction like fabrication, you naturally prioritize:

- repeatability,
- modular systems,
- fewer fragile edges.

That mindset improves maintainability and makes projects easier to scale.
